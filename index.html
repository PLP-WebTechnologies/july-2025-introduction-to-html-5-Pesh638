What the Bible is

It is divided into two
major divisions: Old Testament and New Testament

Old Testament

Tells of the covenant God made with Israel and events preceding the following it.
Shows how Israel responded
Shows how God responded to their treatment of the covenant, i.e., how he responded when they were obedient and when they were unfaithful.

New Testament

A fulfilling of the od covenant through the sacrifice of Jesus Christ
